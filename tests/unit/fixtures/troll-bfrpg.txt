Troll
Armor Class: 16
Hit Dice: 6+3*
No. of Attacks: 2 claws/1 bite
Damage: 1d6/1d6/1d10
Movement: 40'
No. Appearing: 1d8, Wild 1d8, Lair 1d8
Save As: Fighter 6
Morale: 10 (8)
Treasure Type: D
XP: 555
Trolls are horrid carnivores found in all climes, from arctic wastes to tropical jungles. They regenerate 1 hit point per round starting 3 rounds after being damaged. Fire and acid damage cannot be regenerated. Severed limbs continue to fight independently. Trolls are immune to normal weapon damage that is not fire or acid based. They have darkvision to 90 feet and can surprise on a 1-3 on 1d6 in dark conditions.
